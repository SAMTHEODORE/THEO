Refund & Cancellation

Clear, fair policies for subscriptions, invoices, and data

This policy explains refund eligibility, cancellation procedures, data retention after cancellation, trial terms, and how we handle GST invoices.

Last updated
August 10, 2025
Questions? support@brosonetech.com

1. Refund Eligibility

  • Subscription fees:
    Refunds are available within 7 days of first-time subscription activation for annual subscriptions only, provided no commercial use (e.g., invoicing customers) has occurred. Monthly subscriptions are non-refundable after the billing cycle starts.
  • Service outages:
    If we fail to deliver the service for a prolonged period due to our fault, prorated refunds or credits may be issued at our discretion.
  • Trials:
    Free trials expire automatically. No automatic charges occur without explicit upgrade. If you are charged mistakenly during a trial, contact support for evaluation.

2. Cancellation Process

  1. Self-service:
    Go to Account → Billing → Cancel Subscription. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle unless you have an eligible refund request.
  2. Support-assisted:
    Email support@brosonetech.com with your account details to request immediate cancellation. We will confirm via email.
  3. Data export:
    Before cancellation, export invoices, GST reports, and customer data via the Data Export tool. We retain access to export only for a limited retention period post-cancellation (see Data Retention section).

3. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Retention:
    Post-cancellation, we retain your account data for 90 days to allow reactivation and exports. After 90 days, data may be permanently deleted unless retention is required by law.
  • GST records:
    If you request deletion but legal/regulatory obligations (e.g., GST records retention) apply, we will retain required records as mandated by law and notify you.
  • Special requests:
    Data deletion or extended retention requests can be made through support and may require identity verification.

4. Disputes & Chargebacks

  • Contact first:
    Please contact support to resolve billing disputes before initiating chargebacks. Chargebacks may result in suspension of service and additional fees.
  • Evidence:
    Provide invoicing, transaction IDs, and account details to help us investigate and, if applicable, issue credits or refunds.

5. Exceptions & Special Cases

  • Custom contracts:
    Enterprise plans with signed contracts follow the contract’s cancellation and refund clauses.
  • Fraud & abuse:
    Accounts involved in fraud, chargeback abuse, or policy violations are not eligible for refunds.
Quick Summary

• 7-day limited refund window for eligible annual signups.
• Monthly plans billed per-cycle — generally non-refundable.
• 90-days data retention after cancellation.
• Contact support@brosonetech.com.
